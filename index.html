Harnessing Twitter ‘Big Data’ for Automatic Emotion Identification

After providing a reliable emotion identification mechanism with the help of a 2.5 million tweets’ database which comprises execution of two of the most efficient algorithms, LIBLINEAR and MNB(Multinomial naive Bayes) we acquire a reliable system for emotion identification. By this project, we demonstrate that a combination of uni-grams, bi-grams, sentiment/emotion-bearing words, and parts-of-speech information is most effective for gleaning emotions.
